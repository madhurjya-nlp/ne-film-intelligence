International Film Grants Underrated & accessible
⚠️ June 22 - July 2

Doha Film Institute Grants

Qatar · Development: $15K · Production: $75K
Open globally, no co-producer requirement for shorts. First- and second-time filmmakers welcome. Feature docs (65+ min) and short docs. Excellent fit for NE India documentary stories.
$15K-75K →

Hubert Bals Fund (IFFR Rotterdam)

Netherlands · Up to €10K development
India explicitly eligible. Prioritizes projects with strong artistic vision struggling to find funding. Application windows are short (sometimes 1 week). Sign up for IFFR newsletter.
€10K →

Red Sea Film Fund

Saudi Arabia · Multiple cycles per year
Underrated gem — many Indian filmmakers don't realize they're eligible since the fund includes Asian nationals. Multiple annual cycles = more chances to apply.
Multiple cycles →

Asian Cinema Fund (BIFF)

South Korea · Busan International Film Festival
Script Development & Co-Production Support for Asian filmmakers. Script Development for first/second-time feature directors. Co-Production requires Korean partner.
$10K-30K →

Sørfond (Norwegian South Fund)

Norway · Avg grants €45K-60K
Up to NOK 1M. India is on OECD DAC list ✓. Challenge: requires Norwegian minority co-producer. Solution: attend the Sørfond Pitching Forum (November) to connect.
€45K-60K avg →

IDFA Bertha Fund

Netherlands · Development €7.5K · Production €25K
Top documentary fund. Development up to €7,500, production up to €25,000. Verify India is on the IBF Classic Country List. Next deadline ~early 2027.
€7.5K-25K →

Film Independent Labs & Grants

USA · Mentorship + cash grants
Documentary Story Lab and Producing Lab open internationally. Small cohorts (6 projects) receive intensive mentorship. Also Alfred P. Sloan grants for science-themed projects.
Mentorship + cash →

Sundance Ignite x Adobe

USA · $5K grant + Adobe CC + Mentorship
Age 18-25, global applicants. Submit a short film (1-15 min) + project proposal. 12-month Adobe Creative Cloud + in-person lab + Sundance Film Festival access. Set Fall 2026 reminder.

ST-Specific Scholarships Your superpower
⚠️ June 30 Deadline

National Overseas Scholarship for ST

Ministry of Tribal Affairs · Full funding abroad
Tuition + $15,400/yr maintenance + contingency + visa + airfare. QS Top 1000 university required. Max age 32. Family income ≤ ₹6L/yr. 20 awards/yr. Apply NOW.
Fully funded →

Post-Matric Scholarship for ST

Ministry of Tribal Affairs
Covers tuition + non-refundable fees + maintenance allowance for UG, PG, diploma courses. Income limit typically ₹2.5L/yr. Apply via National Scholarship Portal.
Via scholarships.gov.in →

NEZCC Young Talented Artists Award

North East Zone Cultural Centre · Ages 18-30
Specifically for NE India youth. You qualify perfectly. Also explore Guru Shishya scheme for documenting traditional art forms. Monitor NEZCC website for annual notifications.
Check NEZCC →

Ministry of Culture Scholarships

Young Artistes Scheme · Monthly stipend
Scholarships to Young Artistes for advanced training in visual arts, theatre, traditional/folk arts. Being from a tribal community with traditional art forms is a strong angle.
Monthly stipend →

Assam State Film Finance (ASFFDC)

Government of Assam · 60/40 partnership
60% ASFFDC / 40% private producer partnership model. Filmmakers with original scripts selected by expert committee. Monitor culturalaffairs.assam.gov.in.
60/40 partnership →

Cultural Survival Indigenous Fund

Global · Indigenous-led media projects
As a Scheduled Tribe filmmaker, you qualify as indigenous. Fund amplifies indigenous voices globally. Check culturalsurvival.org for current open calls.
Varies →
